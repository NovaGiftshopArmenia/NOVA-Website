Our Beginning

NOVA was born from a singular obsession: to discover uncompromising, rare, and breathtaking scents in a market flooded with mass-produced options. What started as a private curation of the world's finest global fragrances has evolved into a premier sanctuary for those who appreciate the true artistry of niche perfumery.

Shipping & Returns Policy

1. Shipping & Delivery

At NOVA, each fragrance is carefully packed to preserve its delicate botanical composition. We ship both locally across Armenia and internationally.

Yerevan Delivery: 1-2 business days (Free for orders over 20,000 AMD).
Regional Armenia: 2-4 business days.
International Delivery: 7-14 business days via registered postal service.

2. Return & Exchange Policy

Due to the personal and cosmetic nature of niche perfumery, we are only able to accept returns on products that are completely unopened, unused, and in their original sealed cellophane packaging within 14 days of delivery.

If you wish to initiate a return, please contact our concierge team at concierge@novafragrances.com before sending the package back.

3. Damaged or Faulty Items

If your bottle arrives damaged or leaking, please take a photograph immediately and contact us within 48 hours of receipt. We will arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.

Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic billing, contact, and shipping information when you purchase from our collection or subscribe to our newsletter. This data is exclusively used to fulfill your orders and enhance your experience.

2. Payment Security

All payments are processed securely through encrypted merchant gateways. NOVA does not store or have direct access to your credit card numbers or private financial details.

3. Terms of Use

All content, imagery, and text on this platform are protected under trademark and intellectual property rights. Copying or redistribution is strictly prohibited without explicit written permission from NOVA.
